Frequently Asked Questions

1 _ How can I place an order ???

You can order it online as usual. Although you can email or call us and place your order by telling the product reference number. Beside this you can visit our workshop for retail purchase.

2 _ Whether shipping charges are included or excluded in product`s net price ???

For minor items in bulk, we prefer to include it in product`s net price. As per the case of larger items, it is excluded. Actually, shipment charges becomes excluded when the package weight crosses 250lb.

3 _ Do you sell CNC or 3D Printer ???

At this moment, we don`t have this capability. But, for the sake of our customer ease and strong bond, we offer foriegn dealing through which we can arrange it for you at very minimlistic price.

4 _ What if on delivery, something is broken or missing in package ???

In such case, you can concern to our authorities. As per our SOP`s we don`t offer return policy but, for such case we will review the order and send you the product or exchange the item as soon as possible.

5 _ How can I cancel my order if I place something unwanted ???

You have no need to do it. Just reorder you previous one and when our person make a confirmation call to you, just tell him/her. They will do it by their own, no need to worry about it.
