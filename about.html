A Glimpse of Our Journey

Its all started with a mindset of providing world class services and solutions to our native people. We just began with a name of Ardax, A service or so called maintainance providing company whose workers go door to door for work and get paid. It was not as easy and economic in the early days, but as we continued to work hard and gathered experiece, Its all become very deligent. In an very short period of only half year, we had a huge market where people always prefer our work over others because of our high quality work and decent pricing. Now We had the potential to step into next phase of our journey and so as we did. We started a side work of selling parts and components of CNC machines and 3D printers. In the begining we started with a narrow paradigm, but soon we have a lot of Customers. So we prefer to buy the components in bulks in order we can sell it to our Customers with a decent price. In no time we had a huge market where we are providing maintainance service as well as selling our goods with edge cutting pricing.

In a time frame of 2 years, we had a large market, a huge comunity of employees with out class experience and many many more. We had already changed our company name to Ardax Corporation. By seeing all this success, our CEO called a joint meeting of all the company executives, managers and employees. That meeting changed the fate of our company. Our CEO praised everyone`s effort and hard work. He shared a vision to make Ardax stand among one of the world`s top companies. Thanks to our CEO`s vision, a decent maagement of our executives and managers and hard work of our employees, we achieved this visions in no time. With this vision, we changed our buying goods factor in bulks and then sell it to something called self relying capability. That is we started manufacturing our own products and then we sold it out. In the begining, it was very harsh and look like a deal breaking decision. We faced failures, rejections, pointings but we keep our struggle and in a year when we visited a syrian CNC exhibition where we made a number of contracts and partner ships.

Ardax is now more than a maintainance company, as per our CEO`s direction, now we have a brand name of ACMP which stands for Ardax Corporation of CNC manufacturing & 3D Printing. In future we seek to manufacture whole CNC machines and 3D printers and to sell them along with our main component line up. Our journey began with a vision, we started from scratch but now we are included among one of the world best companies. Its all because of the leadership of our CEO and the efforts of workers. ACMP is comprehensive in the production, design, manufacturing of CNC Machine & 3D Printer based products high quality solutions and good efficienct services worldwide.
